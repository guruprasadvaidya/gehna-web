Know More About Me

My name is Gehna Batra, I’m a Freelance Content Writer, Resume Writer, LinkedIn Organic Growth Specialist, and Copywriter.

My ambitious nature is what pushed me to become a freelancer when I was only 18 years old. Since I was committed to becoming the best version of myself, I began developing a wide variety of skills, including content writing.

Nothing comes easy, I worked hard day and night to get ranked as one of the best Freelance content writers at the age of 20. But this is just the beginning of my journey, there’s a lot to learn and explore.
